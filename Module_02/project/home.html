Have shipping needs that you can't cover? JDD Trucking can! Here at JDD Trucking, we deliver to some of the largest cities in the United States, but we also make deliveries along the way. Whether it's one pallet or a full trailer load, we're here to help you in your shipping needs! Our shipping services cover a variety of load types, including but not limited to: produce, paper, equipment, household goods, plants, and much more!

Our equipment is top of the line. We only use equipment that is state of the art and 2010 or newer. Our trucks are all Peterbilts and all of our trailers are Great Dane 53'. With our equipment, we can take care of all of your shipping needs. We are authorized to for 80,000lbs or more in all of the 48 contiguous states. We follow all local and federal DOT regulations and safety is our #1 priority. Contact us for your shipping needs or for more informaiton!
